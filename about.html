About us

Zappes Auto Repair Shop first started in 1980 and ever since then we have been able to continue to grow within our community. We offer full automotive service from checking your breaks to replacing the engine or transimision on your vehicle.

Our team stays current on the latest automotive technology, utilizing professional, hands-on experience working with many vehicle types.
